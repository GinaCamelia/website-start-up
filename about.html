Hi, I’m Gina. I’m a former physiotherapist assistant currently working as a healthcare assistant.

I’ve noticed that my natural tendencies such as curiosity, passion, exploration, creativity, etc., were non-existing in my current job. So, why am I here? Why am I doing this? Why am I giving all of my time and energy away and still feeling unfulfilled?

At the end of the day, my job became mundane and I became more dreadful. Soon after, I have rediscovered my obsession with computers; my "what" if you will. I picked up a few books, went to a few coffee shops to analyse them and it hit me: hours went by and my mind was sucked into those pages. It felt amazing and I took a very big leap of faith - I went online and searched for coding classes for begginers.

Many small businesses were selling their product. I have studied each and everyone of them for months and, only one of them stood out: SheCodes. Why? They had a very definitive and clear motive: they teach busy women how to code in order to work towards decreasing the gender gap in the tech industry. Right up my street. So far so good.

I began learning to code in 2021 and I have been enjoying it greatly. I am developing my abilities to write clean code with every workshop and I'm seeking to continue on this path until I have mastered all of the technologies learned so far.
